STOCKS PREDICTION

Stocks prediction project contains analysis and price prediction. In the analysis part, I analyzed trends and returns of stocks. In the price prediction part, I used the time series model ARIMA and find the best p, d, q to fit the model for predicting the price movements.

1> The 50-day and 200-day moving average are considered to be important trading signals.
2> Golden Cross signal & Death Cross signal.
3> Data Stationarity Analysis: the Augmented Dickey-Fuller Test.
4> ACF & PACF / AIC heatmap finding the best p, d, q.
5> Forecasting stocks price.
