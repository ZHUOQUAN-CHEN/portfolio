AlexNet VS. ResNet18

To compare the performance of AlexNet and ResNet18 models in fine tuning.
Dataset: hymenoptera_data
Epoch: 15

1> Phase 1: The outcome of resetting final fully connected layers.
AlexNet model: training complete in 15m 49s, the best accuracy is 0.888889.
ResNet model: training complete in 20m 60s, the best accuracy is 0.947712.
2> Phase 2: The outcome of freezing all the network except the final layers.
AlexNet model: training complete in 10m 56s, the best accuracy is 0.947712.
ResNet model: training complete in 9m 5s, the best accuracy is 0.954248.
3> Performance:
AlexNet model: 26m 45s, 0.947712, ResNet model: 30m 5s, 0.954248.
