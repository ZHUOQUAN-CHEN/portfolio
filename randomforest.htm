RANDOM FOREST

Objective: using Scikit-learn to test the variation of performance of transforming decision tree to random forest.
Data from LendingClub that a company connects people who need money (borrowers) with people who have money (investors).

1> Exploratory data analysis.
2> Splitting data to training set and testing set.
3> Training a decision tree model, then did prediction and evaluation. Performance: accuracy is 73%.
4> Training a random forestl, then did prediction and evaluation. Performance: accuracy is 85%.
