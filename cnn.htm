PROJECT DESCRIPTION

AlexNet vs. ResNet18 is a Deep Learning project, which to compare the performance of AlexNet and ResNet18 models in fine tuning.
DATASET: hymenoptera_data, EPOCH: 15.

  • Phase 1: The outcome of resetting final fully connected layers.AlexNet model: training completed in 15m 49s, the best accuracy is 0.888889. ResNet model: training completed in 20m 60s, the best accuracy is 0.947712.
  • Phase 2: The outcome of freezing all the network except the final layers. AlexNet model: training completed in 10m 56s, the best accuracy is 0.947712. ResNet model: training completed in 9m 5s, the best accuracy is 0.954248.
  • Phase 2: The outcome of freezing all the network except the final layers. AlexNet model: training completed in 10m 56s, the best accuracy is 0.947712. ResNet model: training completed in 9m 5s, the best accuracy is 0.954248.
